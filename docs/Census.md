# Campaign Census: Dragon Delves

## Dramatis Personae (NPCs)

### Allies & Companions

- **Nezzerem**: A Silver Dragon Wyrmling rescued from the Abandoned Church. Now an ally residing in the party's tower.
- **Echo**: **Saffi**'s loyal, telepathic companion (water-adaptive).
- **The Little Modron**: A robotic survivor from the **Starglass Waypoint** kitchen, now relocated to **The Nest**.
- **Bobson**: A winged kobold thief encountered in the **Starglass Waypoint** luxury suites.
- **Thalamil**: A young elf from **Alamira**'s family, sent to monitor her progress.
- **Orion Solaris**: The hearty bartender/hero of **Greysmere**.
- **Arlen Wymond**: **Caelen**’s father, under guard at **The Nest** for his betrayal in the **Shadowfell**.
- **Cleo Sparkscale**: **Tra’Ahn**’s aunt and a fallen hero (deceased).
- **Uno, Dos, Tres, Cuatro**: The **Modron** caretakers/staff of **The Nest**.

### Guardians & Entities

- **Keliphond**: A disembodied voice belonging to a magical "bag," currently trapped by a dragon.
- **The Spectator**: An multi-eyed, intelligent guardian of the **Starglass Waypoint** library.
- **Melindra**: A disinterested ghost librarian found in earlier adventures.
- **Modrons**: Geometric beings from **Mechanus** encountered in the waypoint.

### Adversaries

- **Zia**: An Arch-Lich and cult leader who nearly cost the party their lives in the **Abandoned Church**.
- **Ylaglan**: A Brass Dragon driven mad by the **Bag of Devouring** at the **Astral Gate**.
- **Uncle Nibblecheek**: A halfling confectioner in **Hobbletown** who turned out to be less than friendly.
- **Ny’athor**: A terrifying shadow-beast residing in the **Shadowfell**.
- **Stone Golem**: The silent sentinel of the **Shadowfell** burial mound.

## Cartography (Locations)

### Major Regions

- **The Troll Mountains**: A dangerous mountain range housing the **Abandoned Church**.
- **Calimshan**: A scorching desert region reached via teleportation.
- **The Shadowfell**: A grim, dark plane of existence (targeted for Session 10).

### Key Landmarks

- **The Nest**: The party's secure tower base, featuring a teleportation circle, alchemy lab, and **Modron** caretakers.
- **Greysmere**: A resilient town that served as the party's early base of operations.
- **The Abandoned Church**: A blighted site of dark rituals and the location of **Nezzerem**'s rescue.
- **Starglass Waypoint**: A Githyanki-built metallic and crystal facility spanning a desert canyon.
- **Hobbletown**: A seemingly quaint halfling village with a dark secret in its candy shop.
- **The Astral Gate**: A high-security location within the waypoint where **Ylaglan** resided.
- **The Shadowfell Burial Mound**: The site where **Cleo Sparkscale** was interred.
- **The Cloakwood Coast**: The current location of **The Nest**.
