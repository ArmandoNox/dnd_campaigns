## Cartography (Locations)

### Major Regions

- **The Troll Mountains**: A dangerous mountain range housing the **Abandoned Church**.
- **Calimshan**: A scorching desert region reached via teleportation.
- **The Shadowfell**: A grim, dark plane of existence (targeted for Session 10).

### Key Landmarks

- **The Nest**: The party's secure tower base, featuring a teleportation circle, alchemy lab, and **Modron** caretakers.
- **Greysmere**: A resilient town that served as the party's early base of operations.
- **The Abandoned Church**: A blighted site of dark rituals and the location of **Nezzerem**'s rescue.
- **Starglass Waypoint**: A Githyanki-built metallic and crystal facility spanning a desert canyon.
- **Hobbletown**: A seemingly quaint halfling village with a dark secret in its candy shop.
- **The Astral Gate**: A high-security location within the waypoint where **Ylaglan** resided.
- **The Shadowfell Burial Mound**: The site where **Cleo Sparkscale** was interred.
- **The Cloakwood Coast**: The current location of **The Nest**.
